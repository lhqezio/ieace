Client needed a secure document search for 5TB of legal discovery data.

Constraints:
- Air-gapped environment
- Sub-200ms query latency
- No external APIs

Solution:
- 3x consumer GPUs (RTX 4090s)
- Qdrant for vector storage
- Mistral 7B for synthesis

Deployed in 48 hours. Total hardware cost: <$10k.
