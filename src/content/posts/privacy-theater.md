"Private cloud" is an oxymoron when it comes to LLMs.

If you are sending your prompt to an API endpoint you do not control, you have lost custody of your data. Encryption in transit is not enough. Encryption at rest is not enough.

Unless you control the runtime memory, you are trusting a vendor's promise over mathematical certainty.

We build pipelines where you hold the keys.
